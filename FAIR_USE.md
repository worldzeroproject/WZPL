# WZP‑SDK Premium – Fair Use Policy

Your Premium subscription grants access to the WZP‑SDK, updates, marketplace,
and developer resources.

## 1. One Subscription = One Developer
A Premium subscription is licensed to a single developer. You may use your
subscription on multiple personal devices.

## 2. Team Usage
Teams and companies must purchase one subscription per developer who uses the
SDK, tools, or marketplace.

## 3. Account Sharing
Sharing your account or API key beyond personal use is not permitted. If unusual
usage is detected (e.g., many users or devices on one key), we may contact you
to clarify the situation or recommend a team plan.

## 4. No DRM
We do not lock or restrict your devices. This policy is trust‑based and designed
to keep the platform sustainable for everyone.

## 5. Contact
If you need a team plan or have special requirements, please contact support.
