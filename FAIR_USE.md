# WZP‑SDK Premium – Fair Use Policy

This Fair Use Policy exists to keep the World Zero Project sustainable, fair, and accessible for all developers. It is designed to be simple, trust‑based, and respectful of your workflow.

## 1. One Subscription = One Developer
A Premium subscription is licensed to a single developer.  
You may use your subscription on multiple personal devices without restriction.

## 2. Team and Company Usage
Teams and organizations must purchase one subscription per developer who uses the SDK, tools, runtime components, or marketplace resources.

If your team grows or you need a custom plan, we are happy to help.

## 3. Account and API Key Usage
Your account and API key are personal and non‑transferable.  
Sharing them beyond personal use is not permitted.

If unusual activity is detected—such as many users or devices accessing the platform through a single key—we may contact you to clarify the situation or recommend a team plan.

## 4. No DRM or Device Restrictions
We do not lock, limit, or restrict your devices.  
This policy is trust‑based and relies on developers using the platform responsibly.

## 5. Violations and Review
If fair use is violated, we may temporarily review or suspend API key access until the situation is resolved.  
We always aim for a respectful, developer‑friendly resolution.

## 6. Contact
For team plans, special requirements, or questions, please contact support.
